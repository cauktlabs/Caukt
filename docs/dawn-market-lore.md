# Dawn Market lore

The hall opens before sunrise. Fluorescent tubes buzz above tiled floors and the air smells like brine and newspaper. Every stall runs its own auction clock, and the ringing bell resets anyone leaning in too quietly.

## Update 4

Side alleys are quieter. Smaller stalls run shorter countdowns but more of them; the crowd thins out enough that a fast runner can hit three stalls in a single decay window.
